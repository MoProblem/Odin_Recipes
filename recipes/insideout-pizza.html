Ingredients

  • 1 tube pilsbury pizza dough or french bread
  • mozerella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Other pizza toppings per preference
  • Pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Roll out dough and stretch it out to fully cover baking sheet. Layer cheese, pepperoni, and other toppings as desired in the center of the dough. Do not put sauce in the pizza. Fold one side of the dough over the center, then fold the other side to enclose filling.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Slice pizza and serve with pizza sauce on the side as a dip.
