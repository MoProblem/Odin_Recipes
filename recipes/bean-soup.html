Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups vegetable broth, divided
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell peper, chopped
  • 2 (15 ounce) can black beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can great northern beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can dark kidney beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can rotel
  • 1 (32 ounce) cartons of chicken broth
  • Limes
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef until no longer pink.
  2. While the beef cooks, drain and rinse the beans.
  3. When the beef is cooked, add the beans, can of rotel and half to the full 32 ounces of chicken broth.
  4. Let simmer for at least 20 minutes, or until the beans are cooked through.
  5. Top with lime juice, cilantro or some hot sauce and enjoy.
